## üöÄ Key Features
* **Performance Metrics:** Tracks Total Sales, Total Orders, and Profit.
* **Customer Demographics:** Analyzes sales distribution by gender and age groups (GenZ, GenX, Y2K).
* **Product Performance:** Visualizes profit margins by product name.
* **Time-Series Analysis:** Displays sales trends across quarters and months to identify seasonal peaks.
* **Efficiency Ratios:** The dashboard tracks critical health metrics: Sales vs COGs ratio, Return Rate, and Repeat Rate.

## üí° Data Insights

* **Profitability:** The business maintains a high-profit margin, securing **$1.95M** in profit from **$2.67M** in total sales.
* **Customer Profile:** Sales are driven by the younger age-groups, specifically **Gen Z** and **Y2K** individuals.
* **Gender Balance:** Purchasing power is nearly identical between **Females (49.8%)** and **Males (48%)**, suggesting marketing efforts should remain gender-neutral.
* **Inventory Focus:** The **Dynasty Winter Jacket** is the flagship revenue driver, significantly outperforming other products like the Pendant Necklace.
* **Customer Loyalty:** While older age-groups, **Baby Boomers (BB)** and **Silent Generations (SG)** have lower sales volume, they exhibit the highest **repeat purchase rate (8%)** in comparism with the younger age-groups.
* **Seasonality:** Performance peaks sharply in **Q4**, with the highest sales activity occurring during **November and December**.
* **Operational Health:** The dashboard shows a stable **5% return rate**, though the **7% repeat rate** indicates room for improved customer retention strategies. Instead of constantly relying on getting new customers, focus also on getting the loyalty of the old ones.
